### Usage Steps
1. **Knock on the Door:** Sign in with your Google credentials.
2. **Establish a Frequency:** Add a new contact to your private file.
3. **Issue Orders:** Speak to Cynthia; she's ready to summarize, categorize, and assist.
4. **Maintain Protocol:** Check for updates in the top right corner.

### Privacy Statement
Side Piece is built on **Local-First** principles. No telemetry, no bloat, and no prying eyes. Your data lives in your private Firebase instance. Cynthia works for you, and only you.
